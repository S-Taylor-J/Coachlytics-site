Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add players?
Go to Team Management → Roster and tap “Add Player”.

How do I track stats during a game?
Open the Game or Practice session and log stats in real time from the Player Stats screen.

Can I manage multiple teams?
Yes. Coachlytics supports multiple teams and multiple sports.

How do I export reports?
Use the Dashboard → Export option to generate shareable performance reports.
